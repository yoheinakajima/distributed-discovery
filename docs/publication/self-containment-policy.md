# Self-containment policy

Every logical dependency of a submission must either be restated with
sufficient theorem statement and proof in that manuscript or resolve to a
stable preprint or published technical source. An unpublished companion may
supply context, provenance, scope, claim ownership, or a shared method, but it
may not hide a load-bearing proof.

`docs/publication/paper-dependency-edges.yml` records dependency roles. A
`logical-required` edge blocks freeze when the target lacks a stable identifier
and the submitting manuscript does not restate the required result and proof.
Background, companion-scope, shared-method, shared-definition,
counterboundary, future-absorption, and synthesis-only edges do not transfer
scientific ownership.

Target-service and venue rules remain authoritative and must be rechecked at
freeze. This policy does not claim that any current paper is submission-ready.
