# Journal-track policy

Journal work is deferred, not abandoned. No journal submission, venue contact,
editor/reviewer workflow, or copyright transfer is authorized.

The track becomes eligible for owner reconsideration after either:

- completion and verification of the claim-grade Agents v1 campaign; or
- completion of the first three preprint freezes plus stable paper boundaries,
  dependency edges, overlap map, and licensing audit.

The owner may separately reopen a paper-specific journal gate. Reopening
requires a current venue-policy, fit, version, license, dependency, overlap,
and self-containment audit. AER: Insights remains a parked journal-track option
only; it has no active submission or contact and restarts only after the
journal-track trigger.
