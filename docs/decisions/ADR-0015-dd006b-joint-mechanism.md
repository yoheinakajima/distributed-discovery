# ADR-0015: DD-006B normalized joint mechanism

DD-006B retains DD-006's two-agent, three-target, uniform-prior, independent
2/3-accurate-signal environment. Transfers combine an exact quadratic proper
posterior score, an observable success or sole-rescue component, and obedience
to the declared differentiated recommendation. Nonnegative coefficients are
multiples of 1/4 and sum to one, preventing scale degeneracy (15 vectors).

The census checks every unilateral joint report/action-rule deviation under both
fixed tie roles in four distinct observability regimes. Transfers are external
subsidies; they are not described as ex-post budget balanced. Results cover pure
Bayesian deviations in this registered class only, not arbitrary mechanisms,
dominant strategies, collusion, or mixed equilibria.
