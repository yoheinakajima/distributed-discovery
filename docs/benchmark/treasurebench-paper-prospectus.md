# TreasureBench benchmark-paper prospectus

Status: prospective and evidence-dependent. This is metadata and a checklist,
not a manuscript, submission, result, or publication authorization.

## Working title

**TreasureBench: A Benchmark for Collective Search under Shared and Private
Evidence**

The first section must identify Treasure Hunt as the playable companion to the
TreasureBench suite. DiscoveryBench may appear only to explain frozen
identifiers, historical routes, or the compatibility migration.

## Admission gate

A paper may be considered only after claim-grade Agents v1 evidence passes
custody, contamination, exact-comparator, independent-verification, claim, and
paper-admission gates. Public calibration and a sealed engineering pilot are
not substitutes.

## Mandatory related-work boundary

The paper must include a primary-source-supported paragraph distinguishing
classical treasure-hunt problems—navigation by one or more agents to an inert
hidden target under travel, time, advice, memory, marker, or communication
complexity—from TreasureBench, where software-agent teams receive shared and
private evidence, select action portfolios, and are evaluated against exact
private, planner, and equilibrium comparators under alternative information
and action architectures.

The complete checklist is in
`docs/benchmark/treasurebench-paper-related-work-checklist.yml`. This record
creates no paper file and changes no paper lifecycle.
