Foundations

A compact vocabulary for separating available information from the protocol that turns it into actions.

Definitions

The basic objects

Discovery architecture

The information structure, action technology, allocation protocol, reward rule, and feedback process that jointly determine feasible search.

Information frontier

The best attainable expected discovery within a declared budget and feasible protocol class under the available information.

Protocol loss

The gap between that frontier and discovery under the implemented protocol. It is benchmark-relative.

Recovery budget

The smallest action budget whose information frontier reaches a declared comparison value.
