## Search outcome

The collision risk is high for any broad claim that information can help or
hurt, that public information can coordinate actions, that congestion can
induce dispersion, that source correlation matters, or that a posterior list
captures more mass than a posterior mode. Those are established themes.

The defensible paper contribution is the composition of a discovery-specific
objective with (i) an explicit action-compression mechanism, (ii) the exact
aggregation-versus-independent-rescue identity, (iii) its residual-error
contraction criterion, (iv) action-budget profiles and centralized recovery,
and (v) one exact decentralized selected-equilibrium gain paired with an
every-equilibrium failure. The review does not establish priority, exhaust the
literature, or authorize a novelty claim.
