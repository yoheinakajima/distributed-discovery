primary-source audit, not a systematic review or novelty guarantee.

The exact candidate is already a one-round posterior-weighted singleton
congestion game. Its sequential version is a finite perfect-information game.
The private baseline and centralized comparator are meaningful program labels,
but they do not enter the equilibrium derivation except as ex-post comparisons.
No eligible information-conditioned statement survives.

Common-information/decentralized-control sources were not used because the
signals are fully pooled before action. Correlated equilibrium, stochastic
stability, assignment, matching, and reliability sources were also excluded
because the frozen model does not use those objects.
