# Registration question

When does a pooled public posterior lead two autonomous agents to cover a
centralized posterior top-two target set, and does fixed sequential arrival
with visible prior occupancy enlarge the region where every pure equilibrium
recovers that set?

The question is evaluated against the named DD-021 direct-private baseline
`P_2(W)` and centralized comparator `V_2^(2)(W)`. It is a registration target,
not an established result.
