two-cloud, one-repeat calibration. Tier 2 is a 50-task sealed engineering
pilot with two cloud snapshots, one repeat, five architectures, exact
comparators, and no claim eligibility. Tier 3 is the blocked 200-task

accepted, and three repeats. Tiers 4 and 5 remain separate confirmatory
options.

The 200-slot base registry is public allocation metadata only: it identifies
families, generator-cell indices, boundary categories, isomorphism classes,
batches, and reasons. It creates no task, seed, prompt, answer, or holdout.
