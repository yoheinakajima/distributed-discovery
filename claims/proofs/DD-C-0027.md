# DD-C-0027 — bounded continuous unrestricted certificate

For a feasible signature and target, conditional success is affine in accuracy: \(s_i(t)=\alpha_i(t)+\beta_i(t)p\). With two searchers, each target contribution \(s_1+s_2-s_1s_2\) is therefore quadratic in \(p\), and so is every complete profile value.

The certificate enumerates every feasible signature multiset for \(M=3,4,5\): 351, 12,880, and 425,503 profiles, respectively. For each profile polynomial \(P\), it checks exact nonnegativity of \(H_M-P\) on \([1/M,1/(M-1)]\) and of \(D_M-P\) on \([1/(M-1),1]\). A quadratic's minimum on a closed interval occurs at an endpoint or, when convex, at its interior vertex. The checker evaluates exactly those rational points. Every margin is nonnegative; equality occurs for optimizers and boundary ties.

This is a complete finite symbolic enumeration over continuous \(p\)-intervals, not a sampled grid. It remains bounded to the three declared values of \(M\), and its status is conservatively checked rather than independently reproduced.
