# DD-C-0040 — the known reversal is not selection-robust

symmetric equilibrium discovers \(5/9\), while every labeled pure equilibrium
chooses two distinct locations and discovers \(2/3\). All global potential
maximizers and every absorbing state of the declared strict-best-response process
are among those profiles, so their selected discovery is also \(2/3\).

P03 reveals whether evidence lies in \(\{0,1\}\) or \(\{2,3\}\), with message
probabilities \(5/8\) and \(3/8\). The anonymous-symmetric message values are

absorbing outcome covers the two highest-mass locations and discovers \(11/15\).
For the second it discovers \(7/9\). Therefore every one of best pure, worst pure,
uniform potential maximum, and uniform strict-best-response basin gives

\[
\frac58\frac{11}{15}+\frac38\frac79=\frac34>rac23.
\]

under the declared anonymous-symmetric selection among the audited procedures.

its lack of robustness to the exact alternatives.
