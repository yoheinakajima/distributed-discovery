# Executed plan

Enumerate the 15 normalized coefficient vectors in each of four observability
regimes. For both fixed tie roles, compute exact report-only, action-only, and
joint report/action-rule margins over all target and peer-signal states. Record
truthful discovery, interim participation, expected subsidy, ex-post transfer
bounds, liability, and best-response multiplicity at the truthful profile.
Verify every row with a separately implemented exact evaluator and reject a
corrupted margin or accounting record.

The run does not enumerate every pure equilibrium of the full extensive game;
it records the exact best-response multiplicity against the truthful profile.
Global equilibrium multiplicity, mixed equilibria, collusion, endogenous tie
rules, and arbitrary transfer tables require separate registrations.
