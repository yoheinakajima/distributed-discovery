# Frozen model

There are three equiprobable targets and two agents with conditionally
independent, `2/3`-accurate signals. Each agent reports one target and, after
both reports are public, chooses one action. The fixed recommendation assigns
distinct reported targets and uses either declared fixed role when reports tie.
Successful agents split a unit prize.

The transfer is a nonnegative convex combination of an exact quadratic score,
an observable individual-success or sole-rescue component, and obedience to
the recommendation. Coefficients are multiples of `1/4` and sum to one. A
component is zero when its input is not observable. Transfers are externally
subsidized and have absolute value at most one; no budget-balance claim is made.
The four regimes are target and actions observable, individual success and
actions observable, sole rescue observable, and target observable with actions
hidden.
