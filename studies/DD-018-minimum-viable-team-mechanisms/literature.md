# Focused terminology boundary

Access date: 2026-07-22 UTC. This note reuses the primary-source orientation
already recorded for DD-016 and DD-017; it is not a systematic review or a
novelty certificate.

Threshold participation and multiplicity are established in Palfrey and
Rosenthal (1984, DOI 10.1016/0047-2727(84)90023-9). Occupancy-dependent games
are established in Rosenthal (1973, DOI 10.1007/BF01737559). Coalition
formation, strong equilibrium, and coalition-proof equilibrium are distinct
objects in Aumann (1959), Bernheim, Peleg, and Whinston (1987, DOI
10.1016/0022-0531(87)90099-8), and Bogomolnaia and Jackson (2002, DOI
10.1006/game.2001.0877).

DD-018 makes no novelty claim for assignment, matching, correlated
recommendations, clubs, threshold rewards, or coalitional stability. Its
project-specific object is an exact, explicitly bounded comparison of ten
institutions under one posterior-ranked threshold-discovery fixture. A passing
unilateral, pair, or exact-size-tau check is not called coalition-proofness.
