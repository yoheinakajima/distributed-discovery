# Frozen policy boundary

There are three uniform targets and initially `N in {2,3,4}` agents. Every
agent observes a conditionally independent private clue of accuracy `p` and a
shared clue of accuracy `q`. On agreement, a deterministic label-equivariant
policy selects the common label. On disagreement it selects the private label,
the public label, or the remaining third label. The resulting three types are
the complete deterministic label-equivariant disagreement class for `M=3`, not
the unrestricted policy class.
