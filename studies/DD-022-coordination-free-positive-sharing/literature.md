# Literature and novelty boundary

Primary-source review is in progress. This registration makes no novelty claim.
The final record will cover team information sharing, correlated signals,
Bayesian congestion and anti-coordination, anonymous mixing, public information,
social learning, information design, and reward dilution. Broad facts that
public information can help strategic agents, congestion can induce dispersion,
signals can be correlated, and anonymous symmetric equilibria exist are not
eligible novelty claims.
