# Model

One target has an initially uniform prior over \(M\) locations. Searcher \(i\) observes \(X_i\), correct with probability \(p\) and otherwise uniform over incorrect locations, and uses a pre-coordinated policy \(f_i:X_i\to A\) without communication. The team maximizes the probability that some action equals the target. Deterministic and randomized policy assumptions will be separated explicitly.
