# Draft treatment screens

- **Acquisition:** free shared clue; paid independently drawn clue; or assigned independent clue.
- **Attribution:** no priority record; or a protected timestamp attached to a verified report.
- **Disclosure:** reports remain private; become public; or become public with verifiable provenance.
- **Timing:** actions are simultaneous and hidden; or sequential with prior actions visible.
- **Reward:** equal split, universal pooling, sole rescue, marginal coverage, or the registered DD-006B joint rule.

Each screen must state information access, action budget, costs, reward arithmetic,
and timing before a decision. No screen is implemented as a live service here.
