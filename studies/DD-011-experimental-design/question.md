# Questions

1. Do participants under-acquire costly independent evidence?
2. Does attribution or priority protection increase truthful disclosure?
3. Does public action visibility increase action diversity?
4. Does disclosure alone fail to create diversified action?
5. Does marginal-coverage reward improve discovery more than universal pooling?
6. Does the DD-006B joint mechanism improve truthfulness and differentiated action?
7. Does source provenance improve interpretation or acquisition?
8. How do acquisition and allocation interventions interact?
