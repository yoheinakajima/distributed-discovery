# DD-002 model

The general object is a designer who observes pooled evidence and releases public signal \(Y\) under policy \(\sigma\), after which strategic searchers play the induced finite equal-split search game. Let \(W(\sigma)\) be equilibrium discovery, with equilibrium selection stated explicitly.

The executed finite model—including the target/evidence likelihood, deterministic disclosure space, payoff rule, complete pure correspondence, anonymous symmetric selection, planner objective, and refinement order—is specified in [fixture-model.md](fixture-model.md). Randomized disclosure is deferred.
