# Focused literature boundary

Access date: 2026-07-22 UTC. This start-gate note reuses the primary-source
orientation already recorded for DD-016; it is not a systematic review or a
novelty certificate.

Threshold public-good participation and equilibrium multiplicity are
established in Palfrey and Rosenthal (1984, DOI
10.1016/0047-2727(84)90023-9). Occupancy-dependent strategic games and pure
equilibrium methods are established in Rosenthal (1973, DOI
10.1007/BF01737559). Strong equilibrium, coalition-proof equilibrium, and
coalition-formation stability are distinct established objects in Aumann
(1959), Bernheim, Peleg, and Whinston (1987, DOI
10.1016/0022-0531(87)90099-8), and Bogomolnaia and Jackson (2002, DOI
10.1006/game.2001.0877).

DD-017 therefore makes no novelty claim for threshold participation,
congestion, strong equilibrium, or coalition stability. Its bounded object is
posterior-ranked competing discovery teams with complementarity below tau and
equal-prize dilution above tau. Exact-size strict-member blocking is a declared
audit convention, not imported terminology for coalition-proofness.
