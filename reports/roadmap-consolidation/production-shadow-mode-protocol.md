# Synthetic production shadow-mode protocol

Status: design-only. This protocol uses synthetic inputs and does not access a
fund, portfolio company, employee, founder, operating system, or production
decision. It deploys nothing and makes no recommendation.

A future registration must predeclare the architecture pair, decision
primitives, materiality threshold, persistence threshold, safety constraints,
reversibility, no-action condition, measurement gate, and publication gate.
The shadow output cannot affect the live decision.

Three outcomes are symmetric and valid:

1. material, persistent, safe divergence;
2. sustained non-divergence; or
3. unstable, apparently harmful, or constraint-violating divergence.

A non-divergence null is a valid boundary result, not a failed pilot. An
adverse or unstable result must remain visible. No real-data or operational
pilot is authorized by this design.
