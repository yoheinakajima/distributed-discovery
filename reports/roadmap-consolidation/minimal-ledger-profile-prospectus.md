# Minimal ledger profile prospectus

The draft profile supplies a small tool-neutral record and synthetic valid and
invalid fixtures. A future conformance suite should validate schemas, reject
unknown evidence categories and extra certification fields, check referenced
artifact presence and checksums, and preserve failed or negative records.

The first exercise must occur on an unrelated synthetic or AI-generated
fixture repository. Passing conformance would mean only that the repository
can exchange provenance and validation metadata. It would not certify
scientific quality, novelty, correctness, peer review, or adoption.

No external project, venue, standard body, or human collaborator is in scope.
