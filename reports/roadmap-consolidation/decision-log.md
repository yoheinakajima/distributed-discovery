# Roadmap consolidation decision log

## Ideas merged

- Reliability, noisy execution, repetition, robustness, multi-target value, and stochastic coverage form Reliable Discovery after a shared value-function interface.
- Latent sources, source acquisition, heterogeneous evidence ecology, provenance verification, and measurement schemas form Dependence and Provenance.
- Randomized disclosure, private messages, correlated recommendations, and alphabet questions form one concrete information-design lane.
- Perfect elimination, DD-015, coalition dynamics, and adaptive coverage form a later dynamic/adaptive synthesis.
- Shared multi-agent-AI context and action collapse belong jointly to DiscoveryBench and Rate–Discovery.
- Signal geometry, incremental pooling, action-budget profiles, recovery budgets,
  source dependence, equilibrium selection, and randomized information design
  form Program V5, the Information Sharing Frontier.

## Ideas deferred

- Discovery Order is foundational-later until signaling, communication, and a decentralized counterexample make it operationally nontrivial.
- Asymptotics wait for a clear finite theorem and declared regime.
- Real-data validation remains prohibited pending ethics, privacy, identification, retention, and governance review.

## Naming decisions

- The research program remains **Distributed Discovery**.
- The academic positioning remains **collective search under dispersed information**.
- *Discovery architecture* is a common noun for the formal object; it does not replace the program name.
- **The Architecture of Distributed Discovery** is reserved for synthesis-level work.
- The public slogan remains **Share the evidence. Diversify the actions.**, qualified by marginal discovery value and circumstances in which repetition is valuable.

## Ordering decisions

- Intellectual priority: Diversity–Redundancy, Dependence and Provenance, then Discovery Order.
- Historical execution priority was Program V4; Program V5; Reliable Discovery;
  Provenance; Mechanism Impossibility; Rate–Discovery; Discovery Order; then
  dynamic/scale synthesis.
- Program V5 moves ahead because it directly generalizes the canonical paradox
  and has a concrete channel family and theorem progression. Reliable Discovery
  moves later and remains intact.
- Randomized Information Design is an early Program V5 lane, not a simultaneous
  branch. Exactly one substantive branch and pull request are active at a time.

- The public thesis is that sharing helps when it improves the group's map
  faster than it collapses the search portfolio.

  declared model supplies an exact identity.

  likelihood ratios, posterior geometry, action rules, tie-breaking, baselines,
  authority, technology, and equilibrium selection remain explicit.
