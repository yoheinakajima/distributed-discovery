# AI lab v2 architecture prospectus

This option designs a future evaluation of research-agent architectures. It
does not change current lab operations, call models, run an ablation, or claim
an improvement.

Candidate architecture dimensions are source independence across research
agents, audience design for shared context, recovery budgets over conjecture
portfolios, coverage-sensitive internal rewards, explicit stop rules,
negative-result preservation, context-isolated verification, evaluator
separation, and matched budgets.

Any future comparison must use DiscoveryBench-style sealed governance:
committed generators, frozen systems, multiple encrypted holdout batches,
immutable outputs before unsealing, fixed budgets, predeclared ablations, and
separate evaluation. Outcomes may be positive, null, adverse, unstable, or
blocked; all remain reportable. Shared model origin and training-data
dependence are explicit limitations.
