# Reviewer-selection criteria

No person is selected or contacted. A future economic-theory reader should be
able to assess information comparison, team decision, Bayesian games,
selection, and social learning. A future AGT reader should be able to assess
coverage and welfare-sharing games, potential games, price-of-anarchy and
price-of-stability distinctions, utility design, and implementation limits.

Readers must be independent of primary authorship, disclose material
conflicts, receive exact scope and evidence links, and be asked for technical
criticism rather than endorsement. Consent is required before naming a reader
or publishing feedback.
