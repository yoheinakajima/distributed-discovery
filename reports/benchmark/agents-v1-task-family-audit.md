# DiscoveryBench Agents v1 task-family audit

Five task families are registered from existing exact scientific owners. The
registry contains 138 canonical generator cells, 58,945 primitive labeled
task-state evaluations, 138 canonical prompts, and 552 permitted isomorphic
surface prompts. No private prompt or task instance was generated.

Counts are derived as follows:

- common-source acquisition: 27 cells and 3,402 labeled states across
  `N=2,3,4`, three accuracies, and three cost regions;
- attention: 27 cells and 9,477 target/shared/private-signal states;
- point/shortlist sharing: 30 cells and 7,547 channel-state evaluations;
- consensus/recovery: 30 cells and the same 7,547 primitive channel states
  under the action-budget axis;
- threshold teams: 24 cells and 30,972 action-profile evaluations.

Every family names studies, claims, immutable runs, information partitions,
actions, timing, rewards, exact private/planner comparators, equilibrium scope,
invalid handling, parameter domain, exclusions, grading, and prompt cues to
remove. The families span acquisition, attention, information sharing,
portfolio recovery, and threshold technology; they do not reduce to generic
question answering or accuracy.

The planned ten public calibration cases and 200 private instances are counts
only. Private instances, seeds, prompts, and answers do not exist.
