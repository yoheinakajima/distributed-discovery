# Agents v1 metric audit

The registry contains twelve scientific/protocol metrics and three operational
metrics. It reports vectors and predeclared paired contrasts; `composite_score`
is null and universal ranking is false.

Exact theoretical comparators remain fractions or exact classifications.
Future agent outcomes receive statistical intervals. Operational calls, tokens,
and cost are not scientific performance. Every metric records numerator,
denominator, zero convention, units, compatibility, comparator, aggregation,
display, and a forbidden interpretation.

No metric licenses human, organizational, provider-quality, safety-
certification, or universal-agent claims.
