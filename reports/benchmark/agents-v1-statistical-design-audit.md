# Agents v1 statistical-design audit

The design is frozen before provider selection or output observation. It uses
paired sealed instances, fixed repeats and batches, conservative missingness,
predeclared invalid-output handling, stratified paired intervals, and a Holm
multiplicity policy. Exact baselines remain exact fractions; model outcomes
remain stochastic estimates.

The design does not authorize significance claims about provider quality,
architectural independence, human behavior, or organizations. The metric vector
is primary; a composite score and universal leaderboard are prohibited.
