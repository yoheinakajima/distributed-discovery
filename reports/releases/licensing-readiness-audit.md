# Licensing readiness audit

Update 2026-07-24: the v0.1.0 content registry maps every included group to
its current license authority and excludes unlicensed third-party assets. The
offline paper bundle can be built and checked deterministically, but external
publication remains blocked on explicit owner attestations for manuscript
source, PDFs, and original generated figures. This is a release gate, not
legal advice or a license change.

The root MIT license supplies clear repository-level terms for original code,
data, schemas, fixtures, site material, manuscript source, PDFs, and original
generated output. The pinned upstream repository also records MIT status.
Third-party bibliographic metadata, quotations, and assets remain governed by
their own terms.

Licensing is **audited but not fully cleared for release or submission**.
Owner decisions remain for the arXiv distribution license selected at each
freeze, manuscript/PDF archival terms, generated-figure and third-party-asset
provenance, upstream bundling, and any later journal copyright transfer. No
license was changed.
