# Canonical-anchor citation audit

The Shared Discovery Paradox is required where a paper invokes the atomic
separation between pooled ranking and a one-answer action rule. Foundations,
Three Results, Common-Source Trap, Incentive to Ignore, Threshold Discovery,
and Information Sharing Frontier use that relationship; Discovery Institutions
is a synthesis index and points to primary owner papers.

The anchor does not own later source-choice, attention, threshold-team, or
sharing-frontier theorems. DD references and external references remain
distinct. A canonical citation documents lineage; it is not a priority or
novelty certificate.
