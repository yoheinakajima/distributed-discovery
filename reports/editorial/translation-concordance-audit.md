# Translation concordance audit

The authoritative registry contains 14 canonical objects and 56 community
formulations across OR/MS, economics/AGT, multi-agent AI, and practitioner
language. Every formulation resolves to live claims, studies, paper owners,
and literature coverage; records its relation type and mandatory qualifier;
and names prohibited phrasing and cleared public surfaces.

The concordance changes no scientific statement or evidence field.
Retrievability, referrer, citation-rank, and semantic-visibility monitoring are
out of scope. DiscoveryBench contamination detection is the only retained
adjacent check.
