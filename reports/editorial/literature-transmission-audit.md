# Literature transmission audit

The registry covers organizational architecture, parallel search, exploration,
transparency, cognitive labor, welfare sharing, potential games, utility
design, information comparison, information design, public information, social
learning, strategic experimentation, threshold public goods, and the internal
attention/sharing companion boundary.

Every required adjacency resolves to bibliography keys, stable primary-source
URLs, named paper sections, citations in those paper sources, an explicit
overlap, and a surviving boundary. This is a structured transmission audit,
not a systematic review and not evidence of novelty, completeness, priority,
correct interpretation, or journal fit.
