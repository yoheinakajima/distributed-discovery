# Publication options rejection log

Under current evidence, the program rejects an omnibus Theory and Mechanisms
preprint, a monolithic theory-plus-benchmark paper, and a web-first or
technical-report-only channel. Foundations as a vocabulary preprint is also
rejected in its current role. Journal-first sequencing is deferred behind the
journal revisit gate.

These are evidence-dependent editorial decisions, not permanent bans. The
machine-readable log records a concrete reconsideration condition for each.
Grand sequel plus technical companions remains a leading recomposition option,
but no recomposition is selected before claim-grade Agents v1 evidence.
