# AER: Insights parked option

AER: Insights is preserved as a **parked journal-track-only option**. There is
no active submission, venue contact, reviewer workflow, or authorization to
prepare one.

The option may restart only after the journal-track trigger: either the
claim-grade Agents v1 campaign is complete and verified, or the first three
preprint freezes are complete with stable paper boundaries, dependency edges,
overlap map, and licensing audit. The owner may separately reopen the journal
track outside the lab.
