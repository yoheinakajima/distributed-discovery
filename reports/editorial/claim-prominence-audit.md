# Claim prominence audit

All 110 live claims through DD-C-0110 have one editorial category. The mapping
contains no evidence status, claim type, source, proof, run, or verification
field. The validator requires exact ID coverage and rejects unknown claims or
evidence-like keys.

The map retains bounded nulls, failed searches, synthetic estimates, and
counterexamples as visible roles. It does not hide them and does not change any
entry in `claims/claims.yml`.
