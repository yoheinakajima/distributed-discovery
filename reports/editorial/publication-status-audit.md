Audit date: 2026-07-23.

The canonical upstream paper is an entry, not an artifact owned by this
repository. The four theorem-family artifacts are working papers; “archival
candidate” is an internal editorial disposition only. Foundations is a
research note. Three Results and Discovery Institutions are synthesis notes.

“published” or “accepted.”

owner. Every public status must resolve to `docs/publication-hierarchy.yml`.
