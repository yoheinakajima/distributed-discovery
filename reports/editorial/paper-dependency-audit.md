# Paper dependency audit

The registry contains twelve directed edges and all eight permitted role
types. Every paper and claim ID resolves to the current lifecycle and claim
registries. No edge transfers primary scientific ownership.

One logical-required edge is intentionally unresolved: *Incentive to Ignore*
depends on a stable *Information Sharing Frontier* identifier or a sufficient
proof restatement after the one-reader-centered restructure decision. The edge
is marked as a submission blocker and the arXiv readiness map holds the paper.
This is a passing governance state, not a passing freeze state.

All other edges are contextual, method/definition, counterboundary,
future-absorption, or synthesis roles. A freeze validator must reject a
logical-required edge unless it is satisfied by a stable target or an explicit
restatement location.
